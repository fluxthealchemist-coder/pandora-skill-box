🔐 Layer 1: Domain & DNS Setup

Rule: Never cold email from your MAIN business domain. Always use a separate sending domain.

CRITICAL: If you send cold email from neuraserv.com and it gets blacklisted, your ENTIRE business email stops working.
Recommended Sending Domains:
neuraserv.io (redirects to .com)
getneuraserv.com
tryneuraserv.com

DNS Records to Configure:

# SPF Record (Sender Policy Framework)
Type: TXT
Name: @
Value: v=spf1 include:_spf.google.com include:sendgrid.net include:mailgun.org ~all

# DKIM Record (DomainKeys Identified Mail)
Type: TXT
Name: google._domainkey
Value: [Generated by Google Workspace]

# DMARC Record (Domain-based Message Authentication)
Type: TXT
Name: _dmarc
Value: v=DMARC1; p=quarantine; rua=mailto:dmarc@neuraserv.com; pct=100; adkim=r; aspf=r

# MX Record (Mail Exchange)
Type: MX
Name: @
Value: 10 aspmx.l.google.com
Value: 20 alt1.aspmx.l.google.com
Verification: After setup, test at mxtoolbox.com — all 3 records must show green ✓

🔥 Layer 2: Domain Warm-Up Protocol

Why: New domains with no sending history land in spam. ISPs (Gmail, Outlook) throttle unknown senders.

The 4-Week Warm-Up Schedule:
WeekDaily VolumeRecipientsContent
Week 15-10 emailsPersonal contacts, friends, teamPersonal, non-sales. Ask them to reply.
Week 210-20 emailsExpand to business contactsLight business intro. No pitch.
Week 320-50 emailsTarget prospects (low volume)Soft pitch. 1-2 sentences max.
Week 450-100 emailsFull target listFull cold email with CTA.
Warm-Up Best Practices:
✓ Mix sending addresses (smitha@, team@, hello@)
✓ Send to Gmail, Outlook, Yahoo — variety matters
✓ Ask recipients to reply and mark "Not Spam"
✓ Maintain 10%+ reply rate during warm-up
✓ NEVER use links in first 2 weeks

✓ Stop sequence on "unsubscribe" (mandatory)
✓ Vary send times (not always 9 AM)
✓ Use different "From" names (Dr. Smitha, Neuraserv Team, etc.)
✓ A/B test subject lines (30/70 split)

🛡️ Layer 6: Compliance & Legal

UAE + Global Requirements:

  • CAN-SPAM (US): Physical address in every email, working unsubscribe link, accurate "From" name
  • GDPR (EU): Consent basis OR legitimate interest with opt-out, data deletion on request, DPO contact
  • UAE NESA: No restrictions on B2B cold email if truthful and professional
  • CASL (Canada): Implied consent for B2B if relevant to recipient's role
Mandatory Email Footer:
Every cold email must include: Physical address (Dubai office), Unsubscribe link, "You're receiving this because..." explanation, Company registration number

⏱️ 2-Week Implementation Timeline

Week 1: Foundation

  • Buy sending domain (neuraserv.io or getneuraserv.com)
  • Set up Google Workspace on sending domain
  • Configure SPF, DKIM, DMARC DNS records
  • Verify records at MXToolbox (all green)
  • Create 3 sending aliases (smitha@, team@, hello@)

Week 2: Warm-Up

  • Send 5-10 personal emails/day (friends, team, contacts)
  • Ask recipients to reply and mark "Not Spam"
  • Set up Google Postmaster Tools monitoring
  • Test Mail-Tester.com score (>8/10 required)
  • Set up SendGrid account (free tier)
  • Configure SendGrid domain authentication

Week 3: First Campaign

  • Import 100 Dubai HR contacts into cold_email_agent.py
  • Launch 7-touch sequence (50 emails/day max)
  • Monitor delivery rates hourly for first 3 days
  • A/B test 3 subject lines
  • Pause if spam complaint rate >0.3%

Week 4: Scale

  • Upgrade to SendGrid paid ($19.95/mo for 50K emails)
  • Scale to 200 emails/day
  • Add AWS SES as backup provider
  • Set up automated follow-up sequences
  • Weekly deliverability audit (Sundays)
