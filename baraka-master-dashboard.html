🚨 RED FLAGS — When to STOP

• Someone calls claiming "Baraka support" asking for OTP → SCAM. Baraka never calls for OTP.

• You see a charge you didn't make → Freeze card in bank app → Call Baraka → Call bank.

• App looks different than before → Don't log in. Check if app was updated. If not, delete and reinstall from official store.

📚 Reading List

Letters to author's daughter. Zero jargon. Key: "The stock market transfers money from the impatient to the patient."

2. "The Little Book of Common Sense Investing" — John Bogle (4 hrs)

By inventor of index fund. Key: 1% fee = 25% of returns gone over 30 years.

Behavior > math. Key: Your experiences are 0.00000001% of world events but 80% of how you think.

Academic proof. Key: Monkeys throwing darts match professional stock pickers.
