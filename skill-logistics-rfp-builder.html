Use cases

  • Route efficiency: Cut fuel cost and transit time with multi-stop sequencing that respects time windows and vehicle capacity.
  • Inventory optimization: Right-size safety stock, reduce carrying cost, and eliminate stockouts with EOQ-driven reorder points.
  • Customs clearance: Classify correctly, document completely, and clear faster — avoiding demurrage, penalties, and client complaints.
  • 3PL governance: Hold providers to SLA, KPI, and performance guarantee clauses with scorecards and quarterly reviews.
  • Reverse logistics: Handle returns, repairs, and recycling with authorization, inspection, and refund triggers that protect margin.
