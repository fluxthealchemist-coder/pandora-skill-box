Your Healing Journey

A 5-session protocol designed for you. Reiki and Pranic Healing work with your body's natural energy to restore balance, relieve discomfort, and bring deep peace.

What These Modalities Are

Reiki is a Japanese energy practice where the practitioner channels universal life force through their hands to your body. You remain fully clothed. The practitioner may place hands gently on or just above your body. There is no massage, no manipulation, and no religious belief required.

Pranic Healing is a Filipino energy system that works with your "energy body" — the field that surrounds and interpenetrates your physical form. It has two steps: first, congested or stagnant energy is cleansed away; then fresh, revitalizing energy is channeled in. Think of it as energetic hygiene followed by nourishment.

What to Expect

In-person: You lie on a comfortable table, fully clothed, with a blanket. Soft music. Warm room. The practitioner works silently or may guide you through breathing. Sessions last 45–60 minutes.

Distant healing: You rest in your own home at the scheduled time. The practitioner connects to your energy field remotely using Reiki distance symbols and Pranic scanning techniques. You may feel warmth, tingling, or deep relaxation exactly as in person. A phone or video check-in before and after the session is included.

After either format, drink water and rest. Avoid heavy meals for 1–2 hours.

Distant Healing — How It Works

Reiki and Pranic Healing both function beyond physical proximity:

  • Reiki: Uses the Hon Sha Ze Sho Nen symbol — the "distance healing" symbol that transcends space and time. The practitioner holds your photograph or name and channels energy to you directly.
  • Pranic Healing: The practitioner scans your energy body remotely using the same palm sensitivity techniques. Cleansing and energizing are performed through intention and visualization, with the same precision as in-person.

Distant healing is equally effective. Many clients prefer it for convenience, comfort, and avoiding travel fatigue.

Common Sensations

  • Warmth or coolness flowing through the body
  • Tingling, gentle pulsing, or waves of relaxation
  • Emotional release — tears, laughter, memories surfacing
  • Deep sleep during or after the session
  • Nothing at all — this is also valid. Energy works regardless of conscious sensation.

Safety & Contraindications

Both modalities are completely safe and non-invasive. Reiki has no contraindications.

Pranic Healing is gentle but more active. Please inform your practitioner if you have:

  • Pacemaker (hands stay 4+ inches away)
  • Heart condition / uncontrolled hypertension
  • Diabetes
  • Recent surgery or unhealed fractures
  • Cancer (a modified protocol is used)

These modalities complement medical care. They do not replace it.

Aftercare

  • Hydrate well for 24 hours.
  • Some clients experience a mild "healing crisis" — temporary fatigue, emotional waves, or slight detox symptoms. This passes within 1–3 days.
  • Rest. Your body is recalibrating.
  • Track your sleep, pain levels, and mood. We review this at each session.

Ready to Begin?

Each session builds on the last. By Session 5, you will have a clear sense of what shifted, what remains, and how to maintain your energy hygiene going forward. You will also learn self-Reiki — a tool you keep for life.
