Phase 2 of 3 · During the Zoom Call

Zoom Interview Script

Conversational questions. Probes included. Jot notes in the boxes.

🎥 How to Run the Call

  • Start warm: "Thank you for taking the time. I'm going to ask some questions about your health and how you're feeling — there are no wrong answers."
  • Use the printed form: If the family filled the pre-call questionnaire, reference it: "I see you mentioned arthritis — tell me more about that."
  • Watch for red flags: Pacemaker, cancer, severe depression, self-harm thoughts — mark these prominently
  • Keep it conversational: Don't read like a robot. Adapt. Skip irrelevant questions. Dig deeper where needed.
Q01 · Opening
"What brings you to energy healing? What's the main thing you'd like help with?"
💡 Is this pain, sleep, anxiety, energy, or something else? How long has it been going on?
Q02 · Medical Background
"Do you have any medical conditions I should know about?"
💡 Diabetes? Heart condition? High blood pressure? Arthritis? Anything else?
Q03 · Medications
"What medications are you currently taking?"
💡 Daily meds, occasional meds, supplements. Don't need exact doses unless relevant.
Q04 · Safety Critical
🔴 Must Report
"Do you have a pacemaker, any implants, or recent surgery?"
💡 If pacemaker — Reiki is safe (hands hover). Pranic needs caution near device. Note prominently.
Q05 · Energy Level
"On a scale of 0 to 10, how is your energy day-to-day?"
💡 0 = can't get out of bed, 10 = fully vibrant. Where are you most days?
Q06 · Sleep
"How is your sleep? Do you sleep through the night?"
💡 Hard to fall asleep? Waking up? Early waking? Using CPAP?
Q07 · Pain
"Do you have any pain? Where, and how intense?"
💡 Location, frequency, intensity (0-10), what makes it better/worse.
Q08 · Mobility
"How is your mobility? Can you walk on your own? Lie flat?"
💡 Cane? Walker? Wheelchair? Needs pillows? Can she lie flat for 45–60 minutes?
— Continue to emotional, energy, and goals questions in the same style —
