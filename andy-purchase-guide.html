1️⃣ Download & Setup

  • Download Baraka from Google Play Store
  • Tap "Get Started" → Enter phone number → Verify with SMS
  • Complete KYC: Upload passport + Emirates ID front & back
  • Take selfie (ensure good lighting, plain background)
  • Submit W-8BEN form inside app (certifies non-US status, reduces withholding)
  • Wait for KYC approval (usually 24-48 hours)
⚠️ Use your virtual card (not main salary card). Set limit to $500 for safety.

2️⃣ Upgrade to Premium ($9.99/month)

  • Tap Profile (bottom right) → Subscription Plans
  • Select Premium — $9.99/month
  • Why: You get 12 free trades/month. You need 5 trades for this portfolio.
  • Standard plan only gives 1 free trade/month = you'd wait 5 months to complete purchases.
💡 Math: $9.99 × 1 month = $10. Much cheaper than paying per-trade fees on 5 purchases.

3️⃣ Deposit $20,000

  • Tap Wallet (bottom nav) → Deposit
  • Select Debit Card or Google Pay (AVOID bank wire — your bank charges $15-30)
  • Enter amount: 20,000 USD (or 73,450 AED — Baraka auto-converts)
  • Confirm. Funds arrive instantly with card. Wire takes 1-3 days.
✅ Your $20K is now in your Baraka wallet, ready to deploy.

4️⃣ Enable DRIP (Auto-Reinvest Dividends)

  • Tap Profile → Settings → Dividend Reinvestment (DRIP)
  • Toggle ON
  • What this does: Every dividend payment automatically buys more shares. Compound growth accelerates.
📊 SCHD pays ~$210/year in dividends. DRIP turns that into more SCHD shares automatically.

5️⃣ Set Auto-Invest ($200/week)

  • Tap Invest (bottom nav) → Auto-Invest
  • Create new plan:
  • Amount: $200 | Frequency: Weekly | Day: Monday
  • Target: Split between SCHD (50%) and VOO (50%)
  • Toggle ON
✅ Now your portfolio grows automatically every Monday. No emotions. No decisions.
