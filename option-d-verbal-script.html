Option D — Verbal Interview

Interview Script

Reiki & Pranic Healing · 5-Session Protocol · 22 Questions

How to Use This Script

  • Print this page and keep it next to you during the Zoom call
  • Ask questions conversationally — don't read rigidly. Make it feel like a chat
  • Use the probes (gray text) to dig deeper when answers are brief
  • Watch for red flags (🔴 tags) — these indicate safety concerns to report to Flux
  • Jot notes in the dashed boxes. Bullet points are fine — don't write essays
  • After the call: Type up key points and send to Flux, or take a photo of this page
Part 1 · Physical Body (Questions 1–8)
Q01
"What brings you to energy healing? What's the main thing you'd like help with?"
💡 Probe: Is this pain, emotional, sleep, energy, or something else? How long has it been going on?
Q02
"Do you have any medical conditions I should know about?"
💡 Probe: Diabetes? Heart condition? High blood pressure? Arthritis? Anything else?
Q03
"What medications are you currently taking?"
💡 Probe: Daily medications, occasional meds, supplements. Don't need exact dosages unless relevant.
Q04
"Do you have a pacemaker, any implants, or recent surgery?"
🔴 Safety Critical
💡 Probe: If yes to pacemaker — note this prominently. Reiki is safe (hands hover), but Pranic needs caution near device.
Q05
"On a scale of 0 to 10, how is your energy day-to-day?"
💡 Probe: 0 = can't get out of bed, 10 = fully vibrant. Where are you most days?
Q06
"How is your sleep? Do you sleep through the night?"
💡 Probe: Hard to fall asleep? Waking up? Early waking? Using CPAP?
Q07
"Do you have any pain? Where, and how intense?"
💡 Probe: Location, frequency, intensity (0-10), what makes it better/worse.
Q08
"How is your mobility? Can you walk on your own? Lie flat?"
💡 Probe: Cane? Walker? Wheelchair? Needs pillows? Can she lie flat for 45–60 minutes?
Part 2 · Emotional & Mental State (Questions 9–14)
Q09
"How are you feeling emotionally these days?"
💡 Probe: Anxious? Peaceful? Overwhelmed? Numb? Let her use her own words.
Q10
"Have you gone through any big changes or losses recently?"
💡 Probe: Death in family? Divorce? Moved house? Retirement? New diagnosis? Last 2 years.
Q11
"Do you feel anxious? What tends to trigger it?"
💡 Probe: Racing thoughts? Physical tension? Panic? Hard to breathe?
Q12
"Do you ever feel low, flat, or like you've lost interest in things?"
💡 Probe: How often? Is it getting better or worse? Any thoughts of self-harm? (If yes, pause — this needs mental health referral.)
Q13
"What used to bring you joy? Do you still do those things?"
💡 Probe: Gardening? Music? Family time? Walks? What stopped?
Q14
"Are you seeing a therapist, counselor, or getting any mental health support?"
💡 Probe: Therapy, psychiatrist, support group, spiritual advisor, or handling on her own?
Part 3 · Energy & Sensitivity (Questions 15–17)
Q15
"Have you ever had Reiki, Pranic Healing, or energy work before?"
💡 Probe: If yes — what was it like? Helpful? Strange? Scary? This tells me how to introduce the work.
Q16
"Do you ever feel subtle things — like sensing a room's mood, or tingling in your body?"
💡 Probe: If she says no, reassure her: "That's completely normal. Energy healing works regardless."
Q17
"Do you pray, meditate, or have any spiritual practice?"
💡 Probe: Prayer, church, temple, nature walks, journaling — anything that connects her to something larger.
Part 4 · Goals & Preferences (Questions 18–22)
Q18
"Of all these things, what would you most want to shift in the next 5 sessions?"
💡 Probe: Pain relief? Sleep? Anxiety? Energy? Emotional release? Ask her to pick top 2–3.
Q19
"Would you prefer to come in person, or receive healing from your home?"
💡 Probe: Distant healing is equally effective. Good if travel is hard. Hybrid = first in-person, rest from home.
Q20
"Are you comfortable with gentle touch, or would you prefer no-touch healing?"
💡 Probe: Reiki can be hands-off (hovering). Pranic is always no-touch. Distant = no physical contact at all.
Q21
"Is there a family member or caregiver I should include in our communications?"
💡 Probe: Name, relationship, phone number. Who coordinates appointments?
Q22
"Is there anything else you want me to know? Any fears or preferences?"
💡 Probe: Open-ended. Sometimes the most important thing comes out here. Give her space.

Post-Interview Checklist — Send These to Flux

Client's name
Top 3 health concerns (Q1, Q2, Q7)
Any 🔴 red flags (pacemaker, cancer, uncontrolled diabetes)
Energy level score (Q5)
Preferred format: in-person / distant / hybrid (Q19)
Top 2–3 goals for the 5 sessions (Q18)
Any emotional red flags (severe depression, self-harm thoughts)
Family contact for scheduling (Q21)

Don't worry about perfect formatting. Bullet points, photos of this page, or voice notes are all fine. Flux will organize and build the protocol.
