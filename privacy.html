Privacy Policy

Nothing is stored. Ever.

Pandora's Skill Box is designed with a strict privacy-first architecture. We do not collect, process, or store any data you input into our skills. Every certified skill runs entirely within your own AI account — ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, or any other provider you choose.

How it works

  • No local storage, cookies, or session tracking on this website.
  • No server-side logging of prompts, outputs, or user behavior.
  • Skills are delivered as plain text instructions you paste into your AI.
  • All processing happens inside your AI provider's infrastructure under their terms.

Payment processing

Payments are processed through Stripe. We receive only confirmation of payment — no card details, no billing address storage on our servers.
