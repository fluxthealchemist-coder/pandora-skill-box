Your First Trade

Step-by-step from KYC approval to owning your first share

Total Time: ~30 minutes · Budget: $100 · Risk Level: Minimal

🛡️ Read Guardrails Before You Begin

🛑 The 7 Non-Negotiable Rules

1
Sleep Test: Only invest money that, if lost tomorrow, you would still sleep tonight. Start with $100.
2
10% Rule: No single stock ever exceeds 10% of your total portfolio. Even Apple. Even Tesla.
3
Emergency Fund Wall: Your 3–6 months of living expenses stays in the bank. Never in the market.
4
No Options for 90 Days: The Options tab in Baraka does not exist for you yet. Master walking before running.
5
Sunday Review: Every Sunday, ask: "Would I buy this today at this price?" If NO for 3 weeks → sell.
6
Profit Protocol: If any position gains 20%, sell 25% of it immediately. Lock in real money.
7
Loss Limit: If any position drops 15%, mandatory re-evaluation. Don't sleepwalk into losses.
🔔
Confirm KYC Approval
⏱️ 2 minutes · 📱 Baraka App
  • Open the Baraka app on your phone
  • Check for an email from Baraka with subject: "Your account is verified" or "KYC Complete" (check spam folder)
  • In the app, look for a green checkmark or "Verified" badge on your profile
  • If still pending: stop here. Wait for the email. Do not proceed.
⚠️
Do not deposit money before KYC is approved. Baraka will reject the transfer and you may lose the transfer fee.

Deposit Your First $100
⏱️ 5–10 minutes · 📱 Baraka App + UAE Bank App
  • In Baraka, tap "Add Funds" or "Deposit" (usually on home screen or profile)
  • Select "UAE Bank Transfer" (NOT card — cards have higher fees)
  • Baraka will show you their UAE bank account details: IBAN, account name, bank name
  • Screenshot these bank details. You need them for the next step.
  • Open your UAE bank app (Emirates NBD, FAB, ADCB, etc.)
  • Initiate a wire transfer / local transfer to the IBAN Baraka provided
  • Enter amount: $100 USD (or ~367 AED at today's rate)
  • In the transfer description/reference, type: "Baraka [Your Full Name]" — exactly as your Baraka account
  • Confirm the transfer. Screenshot the bank confirmation.
  • Return to Baraka. Funds will appear in 1–2 business days (sometimes same day).
💡
Transfer Fee: Baraka charges 0.60%–0.75% on UAE transfers. On $100, that's 60–75 cents. This is the cost of doing business.
Pro Tip
If you have Premium+ ($19.99/mo), transfer fee drops to 0.60% and you get 20% savings. For your first $100, Standard plan is fine — the fee difference is pennies.

Explore Before You Touch
⏱️ 10 minutes · 📱 Baraka App · 💰 $0 risk
  • Tap "Markets" — note the indices: S&P 500, NASDAQ, Dow Jones, Tadawul. Green = up, Red = down.
  • Tap "Themes" — browse "AI," "Halal ETFs," "Bitcoin ETFs," "Saudi Backed." Read what each basket contains.
  • Tap "Search" — type "AAPL" (Apple). Look at: price, daily change, analyst rating, 52-week range.
  • Search "VOO" — this is your first target. Read the description: "Vanguard S&P 500 ETF."
  • Tap the ⭐ star on VOO to add it to your Watchlist
  • Add 4 more to watchlist: AAPL, TSLA, NVDA, Gold — just watching, not buying
  • Tap "Bonds" tab — see what's available. Note the minimums ($1,000+ for most). Don't buy yet.
  • Tap "Metals" tab — see the gold option. Note: "Physical 24K gold." Don't buy yet.
  • Tap "Settings" → "Dividend Reinvestment (DRIP)" → Turn ON
Key Learning
ETF vs Stock: VOO is a basket of 500 companies. AAPL is one company. VOO is safer for beginners because one company's failure can't hurt you much.

🎯 The Main Event — Your First Trade
🛒
Buy VOO — Your First $10 Trade
⏱️ 3 minutes · 💰 $10 invested · ⚡ Real Ownership
  • From your Watchlist (or search), tap "VOO"
  • On the VOO page, tap the green "Buy" button
  • You will see two options: "Buy in Shares" or "Buy in Dollars" — select "Buy in Dollars"
  • Enter amount: $10.00
  • Review the Order Summary screen. Check:
    • Asset: VOO ✓
    • Amount: $10.00 ✓
    • Estimated shares: ~0.02 (this is fractional — you own a piece) ✓
    • Fee: $0 (if using your 1 free trade) or ~$1 (if beyond free) ✓
  • Order Type: Confirm it says "Market Order" (buys at current price). Do NOT change to Limit Order yet.
  • Tap "Confirm Order" or "Buy"
  • Wait for the confirmation screen. It should say: "Order Executed" or "You now own X.XX shares of VOO"
  • Screenshot the confirmation. This is your proof of first trade. Save it.
  • Go to "Portfolio" tab. You should see VOO listed with your $10 position.
🎉
Congratulations. You now own a piece of 500 of America's largest companies. You are an investor. The $10 is tuition — the real value is understanding how the machine works.
⚠️
What you just bought: VOO tracks the S&P 500. When the 500 biggest US companies collectively grow, your share grows. When they fall, your share falls. Over 10+ years, this has historically returned ~10% per year. But tomorrow it could drop 2%. That is normal.

Set Up Auto-Invest (The Discipline Engine)
⏱️ 3 minutes · ⚡ Removes Emotion Forever
  • Go back to the VOO page (search or watchlist)
  • Look for a button labeled "Auto-Invest" or "Recurring Buy" — tap it
  • Set frequency: "Weekly" (not daily — too much noise)
  • Set day: Monday (markets open Sunday night GST, Monday is fresh)
  • Set amount: $10.00 per week
  • Review the Auto-Invest summary:
    • Asset: VOO ✓
    • Amount: $10/week ✓
    • Next execution: [Next Monday's date] ✓
    • Payment source: Your Baraka cash balance ✓
  • Tap "Confirm Auto-Invest" or "Activate"
  • Screenshot the confirmation. You now have a robot buying for you every Monday.
Why This Matters
Dollar-Cost Averaging (DCA): When VOO price is high, your $10 buys fewer shares. When price is low, your $10 buys more shares. Over time, this averages out to a fair price. You never have to time the market. The robot does it. Emotion is removed.
⚠️
Cash Balance Check: Auto-Invest pulls from your Baraka cash balance. If you run out of cash, it will fail. Keep your account funded. With $10/week, $100 deposit lasts 10 weeks. Set a calendar reminder to deposit every 2 months.

Document Your First Trade
⏱️ 5 minutes · 📓 Creates the Habit
  • Open your phone's notes app (or Apple Notes, Google Keep, etc.)
  • Create a note titled: "Baraka Trading Log — July 2026"
  • Copy this template and fill it in:
DATE: [Today's date] ASSET: VOO (Vanguard S&P 500 ETF) AMOUNT INVESTED: $10.00 SHARES OWNED: [from confirmation screen] BUY PRICE: [from confirmation screen] FEE PAID: $0 (used free trade) / $X.XX REASON: "Learning the mechanics. VOO is the safest beginner ETF." EMOTION: [How did you feel? Nervous? Excited? Bored?] GUARDRAILS CHECK: ✓ $100 only ✓ 10% rule ✓ Not emergency fund NEXT ACTION: Set Auto-Invest $10/week. Add gold on Day 5.
  • Paste the screenshots you took into this note (order confirmation, portfolio view)
  • Save the note. You will update it every Sunday.
Why Journal?
Your emotions are the enemy of good investing. Writing them down creates distance. In 3 months, you'll read this note and see how far you've come. Professional traders all journal. Amateurs don't.

Homework Before You Sleep
⏱️ 15 minutes · 💰 $0 · 🧠 Builds Understanding
  • In Baraka, tap VOO → scroll to "Holdings" or "Composition" — look at the top 10 companies inside VOO. Recognize any?
  • Read Baraka's daily 5-minute newsletter (in-app or email). Don't understand a word? Highlight it and Google it.
  • Use 1 Baraka AI prompt to ask: "What is an expense ratio and why does VOO charge 0.03%?"
  • Set a phone reminder for every Sunday at 7 PM: "Baraka Sunday Review"
  • Do NOT check your portfolio again tonight. The price will not change until US market opens tomorrow. Checking obsessively creates anxiety.
🌙
Sleep well. You own 500 companies. You have a robot buying more every Monday. You have guardrails protecting you. You journaled your first trade. You are now doing what 90% of people never do: investing with a system.
